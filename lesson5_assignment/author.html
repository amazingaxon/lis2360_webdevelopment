About the Author

Jacob wants to go into ministry as a full time job when he graduates college, which is why he is so passionate about missions and the message of Jesus Christ.

Jacob Axon is an ICT student at Florida State University. He is a christian who is passionate about the Gospel of Jesus Christ and taking that good news to the ends of the earth. This site exists both as an invitation for belivers who are curious about missions to join in on what God is doing in the world, as well as a demonstration of his ability to code, create links, add images and other forms of media.
